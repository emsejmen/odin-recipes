Banana Pudding

This banana pudding recipe is a creamy, simple dessert that combines layers of smooth filling, fresh banana slices, and soft vanilla wafers. Itâ€™s a comforting and delicious treat perfect for any occasion. Quick to prepare and always a crowd-pleaser, this classic dessert delivers a sweet, satisfying balance of textures and flavors.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pack of Cool Whip
  • 4 vanilla pudding cups
  • 1 box of mini Nilla wafers
  • 8 oz Philadelphia cream
  • 3-4 bananas
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine Cool Whip, pudding cups, and Philadelphia cream. Using a mixer, mix until the ingredients are homogeneous.
  2. Add milk to a bowl and prepare 4 mason jars for serving. Slice bananas into coin sizes, starting with 2 bananas and slicing more as needed.
  3. For the first layer, dunk the wafer cookies in the milk for a few seconds and place them on the bottom of the mason jars, just enough to cover it.
  4. Next, add a layer of the mixture to cover the wafers, followed by a layer of the bananas. Repeat the layers (wafers, mixture, bananas) until the jars are full. Chill in the fridge for a few hours or serve immediately.
