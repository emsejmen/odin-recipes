Chicken Skewers

Chicken skewer with a side salad

For a delicious chicken skewer recipe, start by marinating chicken pieces for at least 30 minutes. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers, optionally alternating with colorful vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and zucchini. Baked the skewers for about 20 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and slightly charred. Serve with a side of rice or a fresh salad for a flavorful meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain whole milk Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp tumeric
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 2-3 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thigh

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, oil, paprika, cumin, tumeric, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and garlic cloves. Mix until combined.
  2. Cut up chicken thigh into 1 inch cubes and add to the bowl. Stir to ensure that the marinade covers the chicken.
  3. Once combined, you can cook it immediately or you can leave it in the fridge overnight so that the chicken marinates longer.
  4. When ready to cook, preheat oven to 450°F. While the oven is preheating, thread the chicken cubes onto your skewers.
  5. Place the skewers on a baking sheet and cook for about 15-20 minutes. Temp the chicken after the 15 minutes to see if more cooking is needed. The chicken should be at least 165°F.
